Official programme for the 1968 Scottish Cup Final: Dunfermline Athletic v Heart of Midlothian, Hampden Park, Saturday 27 April 1968, with the classic 1960s trophy-cover design. FP-0011

England vs Sweden

Programme · 1967-68

Friendly International

Good Available

Official FA programme for England v Sweden at Wembley, played under the lights on 22 May 1968, with a one shilling cover price from the pre-decimal era. This copy has a quirk that collectors of the period will enjoy: the printed date on the cover was wrong when it left the presses, and a previous owner has corrected it by hand in ink to 1968. Light edge wear and creasing to the cover, consistent with its age.
