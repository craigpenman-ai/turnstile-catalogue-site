FP-0300

Newcastle United vs Dynamo Kiev

Programme · 1982-83

European Special / Continental friendly (European Cup Winners Cup era feature)

£11.50 open to reasonable offers

Available

European Special programme for Newcastle United v Dynamo Kiev at St James' Park, 23 November 1982, priced 15p, with editorial on the Soviet champions and their Ballon d'Or-winning forward Oleg Blokhin. A word of honesty on the billing: Newcastle were a Second Division club that season and not in European competition, so despite the cup-tie presentation this appears to have been a prestige friendly - Newcastle won it 2-1, with Blokhin in the visiting side. An unusual Cold War-era fixture either way.
