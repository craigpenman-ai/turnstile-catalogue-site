Dual-team scarf for the Liverpool v Borussia Dortmund Europa League quarter-final of April 2016, with both leg dates woven in. The second leg at Anfield produced one of the great modern European comebacks, with Dejan Lovren's header completing a 4-3 win in stoppage time. The scarf itself is in very good order, with vivid colours and no fraying or staining.
