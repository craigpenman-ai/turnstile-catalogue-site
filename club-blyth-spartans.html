FP-0088

Blyth Spartans vs Wrexham

Programme · 1977-78

FA Cup 5th Round Replay

£2 open to reasonable offers

Available

Souvenir programme from the most famous night of Blyth Spartans' legendary 1977-78 FA Cup run: the 5th Round Replay against Wrexham, moved to Newcastle's St James' Park to accommodate the crowd - 42,267 turned out to watch the non-league side. This is the programme from the match where the fairy tale finally ended, and it remains one of the genuinely sought-after non-league issues of the decade, with crossover appeal to Newcastle and Wrexham collectors too.

FP-0089

Blyth Spartans vs Wrexham

Programme · 1977-78

The Debenhams' Cup Final 1978

£2 open to reasonable offers

Available

Blyth Spartans v Wrexham in the 1978 Debenhams Cup Final at Croft Park, 18 May 1978 - a rematch of the pair's famous FA Cup 5th Round meeting from earlier that season. The Debenhams Cup was a short-lived competition contested by the clubs from outside the top two divisions who had gone furthest in that year's FA Cup, so print runs were small and surviving programmes are scarce. A fitting companion piece to the Blyth cup-run story.
