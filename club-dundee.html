Official programme for the Bobby Geddes Testimonial, Dundee v Liverpool at Dens Park, Sunday 12 February 1989. Liverpool sent a full-strength side and won 3-1 in front of a crowd of more than 9,000. Testimonials at provincial Scottish grounds had small print runs, making this an uncommon fixture for Liverpool away collectors.
