The famous 'We Have Got Kevin!' issue - Newcastle United's official match magazine for the visit of Queens Park Rangers in August 1982, celebrating Kevin Keegan's astonishing signing from Southampton with the England captain and the club chairman on the cover. Keegan scored on his debut in this match, and the programme has become the definitive keepsake of the moment that transformed the club. Vol 6 No 1, priced 35p.
