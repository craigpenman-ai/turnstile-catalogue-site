FP-0718

Raith Rovers vs FC Bayern Munich

Programme · 1996-97

Challenge Match (Stark's Park Opening)

£35 open to reasonable offers

Signed Available

Official programme and souvenir brochure for the challenge match staged to open the redeveloped Stark's Park: Raith Rovers v FC Bayern Munich, Tuesday 21 January 1997. The centre pages carry what appear to be squad autographs from both sides, including signatures matching Bayern's 1996-97 stars Jurgen Klinsmann, Lothar Matthaus and Oliver Kahn. The signatures are unauthenticated and offered strictly as seen - but as a night when the German giants came to Kirkcaldy, this is one of the standout pieces in the collection.
