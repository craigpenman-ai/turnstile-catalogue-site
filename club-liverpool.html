FP-0491

Manchester United vs Arsenal

Programme · 1971-72

Football League Division One

Available

A genuine oddity: the Anfield Review Special Issue covering Manchester United v Arsenal, Division One, 20 August 1971. United had been ordered to play their first two home games of the season away from Old Trafford following crowd trouble, and staged this fixture at Anfield - so Liverpool's own programme covered a United home win over Arsenal. Photographed with the team-sheet page and an original green match ticket for the same fixture. A curious footnote of football history with appeal to followers of all three clubs.
