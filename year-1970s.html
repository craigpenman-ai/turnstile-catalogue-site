Chelsea vs Leeds United

Programme · 1969-70

FA Cup Final Replay

£30 open to reasonable offers

Available

Programme for the 1970 FA Cup Final Replay between Chelsea and Leeds United at Old Trafford, 29 April 1970, published by Manchester United as the host club. The replay - one of the most famously brutal finals in English football history - drew a record television audience of some 28 million, but far fewer fans made the trip north than had been at Wembley, so this edition had a much smaller print run than the original Wembley final programme and is the one collectors chase.

Manchester United vs Arsenal

Programme · 1971-72

Football League Division One

£2.50 open to reasonable offers

Available

A genuine oddity: the Anfield Review Special Issue covering Manchester United v Arsenal, Division One, 20 August 1971. United had been ordered to play their first two home games of the season away from Old Trafford following crowd trouble, and staged this fixture at Anfield - so Liverpool's own programme covered a United home win over Arsenal. Photographed with the team-sheet page and an original green match ticket for the same fixture. A curious footnote of football history with appeal to followers of all three clubs.
