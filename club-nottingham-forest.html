Forest Review official programme for the European Cup first round, first leg: Nottingham Forest v Liverpool at the City Ground, 13 September 1978. The draw everyone remembers - the reigning European champions pitched against the newly crowned League champions in round one. Forest won the tie and kept going all the way to their first European Cup. Pairs naturally with the Anfield second-leg programme, also in this collection.
